Adobo Chicken Recipe

Description

Chicken Adobo is a type of Filipino chicken stew. Chicken pieces are marinated in soy sauce. The dish gained popularity because of its delicious taste and ease in preparation.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Pepper
  • Garlic

Procedure

  1. Rinse the chicken with water
  2. Mix the vinegar, soy sauce and pepper in a bowl
  3. Mince the garlic and put it in the bowl
  4. Put the chicken alongside the rest of the ingredients in the bowl
  5. Mix it and let it marinate for more than 20 minutes
  6. Boil the chicken for more than 45 minutes
