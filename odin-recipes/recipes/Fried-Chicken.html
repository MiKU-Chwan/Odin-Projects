Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

A dish consisting of chicken pieces that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried. The breading adds a crisp coating or crust to the exterior of the chicken while retaining juices in the meat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Crispy fry
  • Flour
  • Starch

Procedure

  1. Rinse the chicken with water
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper
  3. Heat the frying pan and the vegetable oil
  4. Mix the crispy fry, flour and starch
  5. Coat the chicken with the mixed flour
  6. Cook it until the chicken turned to golden brown
