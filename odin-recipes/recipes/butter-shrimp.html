Butter Shrimp Recipe

Description

A super easy dish to make that is rich in flavor and aroma. It is mainly composed of butter as the title suggested and the butter is cooked alongside the shrimp unlike in a traditional makings of a sauce.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Vegtable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Vetsin
  • Chili Powder

Procedure

  1. Minced the garlic
  2. Start the stove at a low fire
  3. Add the vegtable oil alongside the butter and garlic in a frying pan
  4. Wait for the garlic to be cooked and the butter to melt
  5. Add the proper amount of salt and pepper based on how many shrimp you'll cook
  6. Add vetsin and oyster sauce afterwards
  7. Add the chili powder, be careful as to not put too much
  8. Stir the mixed ingredients in the frying pan
  9. Add the shrimp
  10. Continue to stir the shrimp to avoid being overcooked
  11. You're done after all the shrimp turned to bright red
  12. Turn off the stove and enjoy the meal
